CHICKEN SANDWICH

Best Italian Dish

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Sauté garlic and onion in olive oil until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
